A place that comes to mind when I want hamburg steak in a tidier, almost course-style format.

## What Stood Out

- **Omakase-like pacing**: The meal flows calmly plate by plate, which makes the whole experience feel polished.
- **Strong value**: The quality and composition feel very good for the price, so it is easy to revisit.
- **A clear best order**: The signature trio and side-dish pairing make the first visit easy to plan.

## Basic Info

- 📍 Hapjeong-dong, Mapo-gu, Seoul (about 4-5 minutes from Hapjeong Station Exit 8, roughly `54 World Cup-ro 1-gil, 1F`)
- 💰 Moa Hamban for one is 15,000 KRW, and the set for two (`2 Moa Hamban + grilled Brussels sprouts with cheese`) is 36,000 KRW
- 🗓️ Open daily 11:30-21:00, weekday break 15:00-17:00, waitlist available on Catchtable

## Recommended Order

- **Moa Hamban**: A three-way hamburg steak plate with chicken, pork, and beef, so it captures the restaurant's style in one order. It is 15,000 KRW for one person.
- **Hamban Set for Two**: If you go as a pair, the 36,000 KRW set with `2 Moa Hamban + grilled Brussels sprouts with cheese` is the most straightforward and satisfying order.
