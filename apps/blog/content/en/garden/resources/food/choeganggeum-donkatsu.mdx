It is on the pricier side, but the satisfaction level fully makes sense once the food arrives.

## What Stood Out

- **Moist texture**: The pork cutlet is not dry at all and feels remarkably soft.
- **Worth the price**: It is not cheap, but the quality makes the cost easy to accept.
- **Strong full-set experience**: It feels satisfying not only as a cutlet plate but as a complete meal.

## Basic Info

- 📍 Hapjeong-dong, Mapo-gu, Seoul (about 1 minute from Hapjeong Station Exit 8, roughly `31-30 World Cup-ro 3-gil`)
- 💰 Sirloin Donkatsu 16,000 KRW, Tenderloin Donkatsu 17,000 KRW, Premium Sirloin Donkatsu 18,000 KRW
- 🗓️ 11:00-21:00, closed on Wednesdays, weekday break time is usually in place, and Catchtable or on-site waiting is reportedly available

## Note

- There can be a wait, so it is better to go with a bit of extra time.
- Weekend lunch seems especially busy, so timing matters.
