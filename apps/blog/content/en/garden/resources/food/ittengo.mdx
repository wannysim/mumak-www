A ramen shop I want to revisit whenever basil ramen suddenly comes back to mind.

## What Stood Out

- **Basil ramen**: Not the most common choice, but once it stays in memory, it keeps calling you back.
- **Clear personality**: It has a flavor direction that feels distinct from a more typical ramen shop.
- **A memorable Hapjeong ramen stop**: Even with the wait, it feels worth coming back for.

## Basic Info

- 📍 Hapjeong-dong, Mapo-gu, Seoul (roughly `11 Poeun-ro`)
- 💰 Basil ramen around 10,000-11,000 KRW, other ramen usually around 7,000-8,500 KRW
- 🗓️ 11:30-21:00, break 15:00-17:00, closed on Sundays and the last Monday of each month based on the information I found

## Note

- On a first visit, the obvious place to start is the **basil ramen**.
- There is often a wait, so arriving near opening time is usually easier.
