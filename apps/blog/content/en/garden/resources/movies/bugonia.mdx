A 2025 film by Yorgos Lanthimos. A black comedy thriller that twists conspiracy thinking, ecological anxiety, and corporate power into something cold and strange.

## Key Themes

- **The face of conspiracy thinking**: It shows, at an uncomfortable distance, how a belief that looks absurd can still consume a person's sense of reality.
- **Ecology and power**: I find it compelling how private anxiety spills into anger toward corporate systems and environmental collapse.
- **The tension of a remake**: It is especially interesting as an English-language reinterpretation of the Korean film `Save the Green Planet!`.

## Summary

Lanthimos's dry direction seems like a strong fit for material this bizarre. The premise is ridiculous on purpose, yet it still feels capable of hitting the anxieties and obsessions of the present moment head-on.
